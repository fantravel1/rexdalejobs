About Rexdale

Rexdale is a vibrant, multicultural neighborhood in the northwest corner of Etobicoke, Toronto. Known for its diverse community and thriving local businesses, Rexdale offers everything from authentic international cuisine to essential services.

Our directory aims to connect residents and visitors with the wide array of businesses that make Rexdale unique. From family-owned restaurants serving cuisines from around the world to professional services and healthcare providers, you'll find it all here.
